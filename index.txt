                      <h5 class="title-left">
                        FAQs:
                      </h5>
                    </div>
                    <p class="lead">
                      <b>Q. Who can participate in the hackathon?</b><br>
A. Any student with a valid college ID card and a receipt of registration may participate.<br><br>

<b>Q. I have never participated in a Hackathon. Should I participate in this one?</b><br>
A. Everybody has a first time. We encourage first timers to participate in the Hackathon and get first-hand experience in building a real life product.<br><br>

<b>Q. Are cross college teams allowed?</b><br>
A. Yes, participants from different colleges/institutions may form a team. However, one participant may not be a part of different teams for the same event.<br><br>

<b>Q. What should we bring along on the day of the event?</b><br>
A. Participating teams are required to carry their registration receipts along with their valid college ID cards, laptops and any necessary laptop accessories.<br><br>


<b>Q. Will food be provided?
Will the participants have to pay extra?</b><br>
A. Yes, food will be provided. Participants will be given meals throughout the duration of the Hackathon. Tea and Coffee will be served at appropriate timings.<br><br>


<b>Q. Will internet connection be provided?</b><br>
A. Yes, internet connection will be provided on one computer at the venue. Participants must bring their own laptops to the event.<br><br>


<b>Q. Is on-spot registration available?</b><br>
A. Yes, on-spot registration is allowed. Although, participants are requested to register before the event to avoid any last-minute hassles.<br><br>


<b>Q. Will there be any arrangement for entertainment?</b><br>
A. We will arrange for entertainment for the teams. Hope you like some soccer games.<br><br>


<b>Q. Can one participant form a team?</b><br>
A. Yes, individual participation will be allowed but it is advised that at least two participants form a team.
